About Us

Bleam Luxe is a brand that specializes in made-to-order clothing, offering a diverse selection of styles and exquisite designs. Founded in 2022 by Akingboye Janet Opeyemi.our collections include casual, corporate/formal wear, and seasonal pieces. Bleam Luxe is committed to providing high-quality made-to-order clothing that is tailored to fit each customer's unique measurements and preferences. With a focus on customization, customers can choose from a wide range of fabrics, colors, and styles to create a truly one-of-a-kind garment that reflects their individuality and personal style. We prioritize sustainable and ethical production practices by using eco-friendly materials, all in the pursuit to ELEVATE YOUR STYLE
